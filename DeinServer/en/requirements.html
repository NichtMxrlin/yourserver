Application requirements

Here you can apply as a team member. Read everything carefully and take at least 10 minutes to apply. If you have not received an answer after 7 days, you are rejected. If we like your application, we will contact you via Discord and hold an interview. During the interview you then have to use the (DeinServer application system).


➥ What we offer you : » Systems for the detection of cheaters,
» A detailed introduction and help from selected team members,
» A future-oriented team,
» All rights of a premium rank and many more in the game,

➥ What to bring :
» You should be at least 13 years old,
» You should bring enough activity with you (at least 10-15 hours per week),
» You should have a basic knowledge of English,
» You should have a comfortable microphone quality,
» You should be familiar with Minecraft & Discord,
» You should have good spelling.


Please note:
You are not the only one applying, which is why we unfortunately cannot respect every application. Make your application unique and stand out from the others. Good luck with love, the DeinServer.net team !! ❤️
